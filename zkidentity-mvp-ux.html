How We Protect Your Privacy

Verified Securely

Your data is checked inside a protected environment. Nobody — not even us — can see your personal information during verification.

Personal Data Deleted

Your name, date of birth, photos, and biometrics are checked then permanently deleted. They are never saved anywhere.

Tamper-Proof Record

Only your verified status is saved: which provider, your country, and that you passed. Nothing personal — ever.
